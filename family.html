Family

Ariana Copeland

My family includes my Mother, Step-Father, Two Half-Siblings, and one Step Sister, and my Grandfather

The only grandparents I have met were my mother's parents, or as I called them Mommaw and Poppaw. They both were raised in West Virgina and moved to Georgia when my mother was a teen. My mother had two fathers growing up becuase my grandmother remarried 4 times. Only two of those men matter Poppaw and Popaw Shuck.(See how creative we were...lol) For most of my life we did not meet many of our relatives from out of state. My Great Aunt Pam moved here with her children, my uncles, the same time my Mommaw did.

When my mother got married in 2014 I gained an even larger family, and most importantly an amazing step-dad who has done so much for us.
